#+title: (en.wikipedia.org) The Wiki Way - Wikipedia
#+filetags: :information_management:www:books:website:

#+begin_quote
  /*The Wiki Way: Quick Collaboration on the Web*/ is a 2001 book about [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wiki][wikis]] by [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bo_Leuf][Bo Leuf]] and [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ward_Cunningham][Ward Cunningham]].

  It was the first major book published about using wikis.  Cunningham invented wikis when he wrote [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WikiWikiWeb][WikiWikiWeb]], the first wiki website software.

  The book is about how to manage wiki systems, followed by a perspective on the nature of wiki-style online communication.
#+end_quote
