#+begin_quote
  *Peter John Landin* (5 June 1930 -- 3 June 2009) was a British [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_scientist][computer scientist]].  He was one of the first to realise that the [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lambda_calculus][lambda calculus]] could be used to model a [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Programming_language][programming language]], an insight that is essential to the development of both [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Functional_programming][functional programming]] and [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Denotational_semantics][denotational semantics]].
#+end_quote
