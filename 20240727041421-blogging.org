#+title: Blogging
#+filetags: :www:writing:blogging:

Blogs, short for [[id:05f2a17c-4ceb-42fa-bcc8-16d61473804b][web]] logs, are online publication usually by a single individual and generally amateur rather than professional.  Often, blogging takes to form of serialized [[id:5ee4322f-6a78-4132-9a38-a3e81698c383][essay]] writing.
