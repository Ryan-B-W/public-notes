  * Organic Maps: Offline Hike, Bike, Trails and Navigation

  *Organic Maps* is a free Android & iOS offline maps app for travelers, tourists, hikers, drivers and cyclists based on *[[https://www.openstreetmap.org/about][OpenStreetMap]]* data created by the community.  It is a privacy-focused, open-source [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fork_(software_development)][fork]] of *Maps.me* app (previously known as [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maps.me][*MapsWithMe*]]), maintained by the same people who created *MapsWithMe* in 2011.

  *Organic Maps* is one of the few applications nowadays that supports 100% of features without an active Internet connection.  Install Organic Maps, download maps, throw away your SIM card, and go for a weeklong trip on a single battery charge without any byte sent to the network.

  : In 2023, Organic Maps [[https://organicmaps.app/news/2023-12-23/in-2023-organic-maps-got-its-first-million-users/][got its first million]] users. [[https://organicmaps.app/donate/][Help us]] to scale!

  ** Features

  Organic Maps is the ultimate companion app for travelers, tourists, hikers, and cyclists:

  - Detailed offline maps with places that don't exist on other maps, thanks to [[https://www.openstreetmap.org/about][OpenStreetMap]]
  - Cycling routes, hiking trails, and walking paths
  - Contour lines, elevation profiles, peaks, and slopes
  - Turn-by-turn walking, cycling, and car navigation with voice guidance and Android Auto
  - Fast offline search on the map
  - Export/import bookmarks in KML/KMZ, import GPX
  - Dark Mode to protect your eyes
  - Countries and regions don't take a lot of space
  - Free and open-source
