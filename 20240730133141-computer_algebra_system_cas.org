#+title: Computer Algebra System (CAS)
#+filetags: :mathematics:programming:computer_science:

A piece of software that can do symbolic algebraic manipulations and calculations instead of the simpler rational arithmetic of simple calculators.

Common examples:
 - [[id:95ed9369-26d3-4050-b079-016f3114ba9a][Maxima]], an open source CAS written in [[id:b5fb5c4b-d10f-4bca-91e1-a5e946ef0c83][Common Lisp]]
 - Mathematica, a proprietary CAS and simulation suite from Wolfram
 - Symbolica, a proprietary CAS library
 - MATLAB, a proprietary mathematics and simulation suite that includes the Symbolic Math Toolbox CAS
 - Wolfram Alpha, a proprietary online CAS from Wolfram
