#+begin_quote
  The *TI-Nspire* is a [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graphing_calculator][graphing calculator]] line made by [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Texas_Instruments][Texas Instruments]], with the first version released on 25 September 2007.  The calculators feature a non-[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/QWERTY][QWERTY]] keyboard and a different key-by-key layout than Texas Instruments's previous flagship calculators such as the [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TI-89_series][TI-89 series]].
