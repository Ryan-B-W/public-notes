  The *Jerusalem artichoke* (/*Helianthus tuberosus*/), also called *sunroot*, *sunchoke*, *wild sunflower*, *topinambur*, or *earth apple*, is a species of [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Helianthus][sunflower]] native to central [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_America][North America]].  It is cultivated widely across the temperate zone for its [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tuber][tuber]], which is used as a [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Root_vegetable][root vegetable]].
