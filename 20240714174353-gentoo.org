#+title: Gentoo

A source-based [[id:bf0bc2d7-17df-413c-823b-93904faffc58][Linux]] distribution.  Mostly known for providing a great deal of choice, not making default assumptions of the user's preferences where possible, and being a very cutting edge rolling release distribution given its source-based nature.
