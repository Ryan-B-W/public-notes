#+begin_quote
  /*The SAS Survival Handbook*/ is a survival guide by [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom][British]] [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Author][author]] and soldier, [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lofty_Wiseman][John Wiseman]], first published by [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Collins_(publisher)][Williams Collins]] in 1986.  Second, revised edition came out in 2009.  A digital [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mobile_app][app]] for smartphones based on the book is also available.  The book spans over 11 sections, and an introduction and postscript, detailing how to survive in dangerous surroundings.
