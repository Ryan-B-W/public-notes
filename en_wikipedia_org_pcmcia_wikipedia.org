#+begin_quote
  The *Personal Computer Memory Card International Association* (*PCMCIA*) was an industry [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Consortium][consortium]] of [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_computer_hardware_manufacturers][computer hardware manufacturers]] from 1989 to 2009.  Starting with the [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_Card][PCMCIA card]] in 1990 (the name later simplified to /PC Card/), it created various standards for peripheral interfaces designed for [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laptop][laptop]] computers.
#+end_quote
