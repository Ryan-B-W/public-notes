#+begin_quote
  * laminar

  Continuous Integration the Light Way

  ** fast · lightweight · open source · self-hosted

  ** Less is more

  Laminar helps you automate and track jobs, and presents the results in a simple and clear interface.

  Laminar encourages leveraging existing tools in your Linux environment rather than reinventing the wheel.

  No clicky web UIs that churn out unreadable XML, no assumptions about your source control or build system; just write a script and Laminar will execute it.

  ** Documentation

  Learn to setup and master Laminar: [[https://laminar.ohwg.net/docs.html][User Manual]]

  ** Download

  Debian 9 (Stretch) & 10 (Buster): amd64 binaries available on [[https://github.com/ohwgiles/laminar/releases][releases]] page

  CentOS 7 & 8: x86_64 binaries available on [[https://github.com/ohwgiles/laminar/releases][releases]] page

  Armbian 9 (Stretch) & 10 (Buster): armhf binaries available on [[https://github.com/ohwgiles/laminar/releases][releases]] page
