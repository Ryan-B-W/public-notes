#+title: Nix
#+filetags: :computer_science:operating_systems:package_management:software_distributions:linux:unix:

The Nix package manager.  Uses the Nix configuration language to manage packages and system or user configurations.  Inspired the BSD ports system.  Purports to provide a fully reproducible means of configuring systems.  Primarily used by [[id:30ae24e9-8b87-4412-ae94-484b5c72216b][NixOS]].  Works on top of most major operating systems including [[id:bf0bc2d7-17df-413c-823b-93904faffc58][Linux]], *BSD, Mac OS, and Windows (via WSL2).
