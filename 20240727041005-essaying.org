#+title: Essaying
#+filetags: :writing:

A short piece of writing on a single subject, generally expressing an opinion on it.

Useful outside of academia for self directed learning and research as a form of [[id:fd886692-0c9b-4878-9484-5f8feb6871a2][learning by teaching]].

Often, [[id:5d05853c-20b4-426d-9309-0eec949e5cd6][Blogging]] can be considered a form of this.
