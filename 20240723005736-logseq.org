It was inspired by [[id:b83f6ef7-85bc-4465-ac86-94fe8d3efbec][Roam Research]], [[id:5ab4a0b0-95e2-40cf-b924-709db3115d48][Org Mode]], [[id:7cfbbdee-d0eb-47ae-8a94-d8c5aea6979c][TiddlyWiki]], and others.
